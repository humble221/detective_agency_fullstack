Employee Screening

"Employee Screening" service by our detective agency is a comprehensive set of measures aimed at ensuring the safety and loyalty of a company's workforce through thorough checks of both current and prospective employees.

This service allows businesses to minimize risks associated with corporate theft, fraud, leaking of confidential information, professional incompetence, and other negative internal factors that can cause severe financial or reputational damage.

When is Employee Screening Required?

Critical Positions

When hiring new personnel for highly responsible, financial, or strategic positions within the company.

Promotions & Management

When appointing current employees to managerial roles or upgrading their access level to sensitive data.

Internal Suspicions

When an employee is suspected of illegal activities, corporate sabotage, or malicious actions against the business.

Data Leaks

In cases of confidential corporate data breaches, intellectual property leaks, or trade secret exposure.

Workplace Conflicts

When severe conflict situations, corporate disputes, or insider threats arise among the team members.

Security Upgrades

As a routine preventative measure to overall enhance the internal security and risk mitigation standards of the enterprise.

What Does the Screening Process Include?

Initial Consultation

Defining the specific goals and tasks of the check, agreeing on the strategy, and selecting proper legal methods.

Data Gathering

Extracting data from various resources: official registries, social media, mass media, archives, and past records.

Data Analysis

Performing a thorough evaluation of all compiled insights to uncover potential hidden risks, background red flags, and internal threats.

Staff Interviews

Conducting professional interviews and targeted background conversations with employees (exclusively with their official consent).

Field Operations

Executing covert surveillance measures within legal limits to identify workplace discipline violations or illicit actions.

Comprehensive Report

Compiling a highly detailed final report containing all verified findings, precise risk assessments, and tactical advice.

Types of Employee Background Checks

• Pre-employment Background Check: Thorough auditing of a job candidate's qualifications, criminal record absence, education validity, credit history, and potential criminal links.

• Routine Screening of Current Staff: Periodic loyalty and security audits to proactively detect hidden operational risks, external corporate collusion, or disciplinary violations.

• Exit Interview & Screening: Investigating the underlying reasons for a key employee's resignation to prevent confidential information leaks or competitive sabotage.

• Polygraph Testing (Lie Detector): Evaluating the fundamental honesty, loyalty, and competence of staff members (conducted strictly under voluntary consent and regional legal compliance).

• Business Reputation Auditing: Gathering discrete insights regarding an employee's professional standing, past corporate relationships, and behavioral ethics within their professional industry.
