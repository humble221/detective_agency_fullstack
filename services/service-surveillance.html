Surveillance Services

"Surveillance Services" by our detective agency provide covert and professional monitoring of a target's actions (an individual, a group of people, a vehicle, or a specific location) to obtain critical insights required by the client.

All operations are conducted in strict compliance with legal frameworks and human rights, utilizing advanced technical equipment and experienced specialists. It is an effective tool to confirm or disprove suspicions, gather solid evidence, and make well-founded decisions.

When is Surveillance Required?

Infidelity Investigation

Identifying facts and gathering irrefutable proof regarding a partner's or spouse's marital unfaithfulness.

Employee Screening

Detecting dishonest employees involved in corporate theft, financial fraud, or leaking sensitive commercial secrets.

Debtors & Fraudsters

Locating individuals who are intentionally evading debt repayments or have committed fraudulent actions.

Teenager Control

Ensuring the personal safety of children and teenagers, and identifying negative or dangerous influences from peers.

Business Partner Check

Uncovering hidden connections, real lifestyles, and actual intentions of potential or current business partners.

Personal Security

Detecting external surveillance facts against the client and mitigating potential threats to personal safety.

What Does the Surveillance Process Include?

Initial Consultation

Defining the specific goals and tasks of surveillance, agreeing on the strategy, and selecting proper methods.

Plan Development

Determining the optimal routes, locations, timing, and tactical methods for conducting surveillance operations.

Covert Monitoring

Conducting professional, undetected observation without drawing any attention to the investigators or the target.

Photo & Video Evidence

Capturing high-quality photo and video materials that officially document the target's movements and activities.

Data Analysis

Deep processing of all gathered insights, identifying behavioral patterns, and cross-checking important facts.

Detailed Reporting

Compiling a comprehensive final report with attached photo/video materials and professional analytical conclusions.

Methods & Technical Equipment

• Foot Surveillance: Discrete tracking of the target's movements while they are walking in public spaces.

• Vehicle Surveillance: Professional mobile tracking using cars, motorcycles, or other vehicles.

• Stationary Observation: Monitoring the target from a fixed, secure point (e.g., from a vehicle, cafe, or building).

• Technical Equipment: Deployment of advanced cameras, GPS trackers, and audio monitoring (within legal limits and with appropriate permissions).

• Cyber Surveillance: Monitoring the target's open online activities, social networks, and digital footprints safely and confidentially.
