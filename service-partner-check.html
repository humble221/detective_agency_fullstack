Partner Verification

"Partner Verification" service by our detective agency is a comprehensive precautionary measure aimed at reducing risks when establishing new business relationships.

It allows you to obtain objective and reliable information about potential partners, minimizing the likelihood of fraud, financial losses, and reputational damage.

When is Partner Verification Required?

Major Deals

Essential to perform deep due diligence before signing high-value contracts or closing major deals.

New Markets

Crucial when expanding your business and entering unfamiliar regional or international markets.

Long-term Relations

Recommended when establishing long-term, strategic business partnerships and joint ventures.

Mergers & Acquisitions

Vital during M&A corporate procedures to uncover any hidden liabilities of the target company.

Attracting Investments

Necessary when fundraising or onboarding new investors into your business ecosystem.

Foreign Entities

Important when dealing with foreign companies to verify their legal status and actual background.

What Does the Verification Process Include?

Identity & Data Check

Verification of corporate registration data, constitutional documents, office locations, and individual IDs.

Financial Audit

Analysis of financial stability, credit history, debt loads, asset ownership, and potential bankruptcy risks.

Reputation Review

Gathering business reputation insights, tracking customer reviews, and identifying past conflict or scandal involvement.

Affiliation & Links

Uncovering hidden connections between executives, proxy owners, and checking for potential conflicts of interest.

Media & OSINT

Monitoring mentions in mass media, corporate registers, social networks, and identifying compromising materials.

Legal Background

Checking for ongoing or past lawsuits, legal disputes, corporate fraud indicators, and illicit connections.

Service Outcome

• Comprehensive Report: Based on the verification results, our detective agency provides a highly detailed, professional report.

• Risk Analysis: The final documentation contains a deep breakdown of identified operational, financial, and legal risks.

• Actionable Guidance: Every report includes strategic recommendations from our investigators on how to proceed safely with the partner.
