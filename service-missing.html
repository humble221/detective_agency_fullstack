Missing Persons Search

"Missing Persons Search" service is one of the most demanded and complex tasks performed by detective agencies.

It involves locating missing individuals, people hiding from liabilities, relatives who lost contact, debtors, and other persons whose whereabouts need to be established rapidly.

When Might This Service Be Required?

Search for the missing

This is the most critical case when a person suddenly disappears and their whereabouts are unknown. It is vital to contact a detective agency as quickly as possible to maximize the chances of a successful search.

Locating relatives & friends

It often happens that people lose touch with loved ones due to relocations, name changes, or complex life situations. A detective agency will help restore lost connections.

Tracking down debtors

If you need to recover a debt and the debtor is hiding, a private investigator can help establish their current location and workplace.

Finding witnesses

In some cases, it is necessary to locate witnesses of certain events or incidents to resolve legal issues and protect your interests.

Biological parents or children

Matters of establishing family ties, including the search for biological parents or children who were separated years ago, also fall under this category.

Biography verification

In specific situations, before marriage or hiring a crucial employee, a thorough check of the person's biography is required, including establishing their actual place of residence and social circle.

What Does the Investigation Process Include?

Data Collection & Analysis

Detectives gather all available data about the missing person: personal details, photographs, connections, last known location, habits, etc.

Database Operations

The agency utilizes specialized databases to obtain intelligence regarding passenger transits, property, and official registrations.

Interviews & Inquiries

Investigators interview individuals who might have seen the missing person or possess any relevant clues about their whereabouts.

Surveillance Operations

In certain scenarios, if there are reasons to believe the person is in a specific area, physical surveillance is conducted to confirm the exact location.

Advanced Technology

Depending on the case, various modern tech solutions are applied, such as geolocation tracking and social media data analysis.

Law Enforcement Cooperation

If necessary, the detective agency cooperates with the police and other official bodies to exchange information and coordinate actions.
