Infidelity Investigation

"Infidelity Investigation" service is a delicate and confidential way to get answers to agonizing questions about a partner's loyalty.

Detective agencies offer this service to those who experience suspicions but do not possess sufficient evidence to make a final decision.

When Might This Service Be Required?

Suspicious behavior

Changes in habits, hobbies, appearance, working late, and secrecy in using phones or computers can trigger deep suspicions.

Changes in intimacy

A decline in interest, avoiding closeness, or completely new intimate practices that the partner never mentioned before can be signs of cheating.

Intuitive feelings

Sometimes suspicions arise without obvious reasons, based purely on intuition and a feeling of dishonesty from the partner.

New acquaintances

Active communication with new people whom the partner does not talk about can cause serious concern.

Past experience

If you or your partner had a past experience of infidelity, it might trigger new suspicions in current relationships.

Crucial life decisions

Before getting married or planning a child, some people want to ensure their partner's loyalty to secure their future.

What Does the Investigation Process Include?

Intelligence Gathering

Detectives collect initial data about the partner, including daily routine, contacts, hobbies, habits, and places they visit frequently.

Physical Surveillance

The primary method to establish who the partner meets, how they spend time, and whether there are signs of romantic relationships.

Social Media Analysis

Detectives analyze social media activity, public posts, interactions, and other open data that might point to infidelity. Access to private accounts without permission is illegal and not conducted.

Phone Activity Checks

Analyzing calls and messages via legal methods like billing analysis with the client's consent to detect suspicious contacts.

Photo & Video Evidence

During surveillance, investigators capture high-quality photos and videos that serve as irrefutable proof of loyalty or betrayal.

Device Security Audit

Analyzing history logs and installed apps can reveal hidden activity. Device hacking without owner consent is strictly prohibited.

Special Methods (Sting Operations)

• Bait & Provocation Tests: This method is used rarely and strictly with the client's consent. A specially trained operative attempts to initiate contact with the partner to test their reaction. This method can be ethically complex, so it must be thoroughly discussed with the detective agency beforehand.
